SGLT2 Inhibitors and GLP-1 Receptor Agonists

In patients with CCD who have types 2 diabetes, the use of either an SGLT2 inhibitor or a GLP-1 receptor agonist with proven cardiovascular benefit is recommended to reduce the risk of MACE (COR 1/ LOE A).
